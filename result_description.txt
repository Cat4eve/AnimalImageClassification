We utilized a dataset of 4000 training images (400 images per class) along with 1000 validation images (100 images per class) to train our model.
The training process spanned across 10 epochs, resulting in noteworthy outcomes.
Upon completion of the final epoch, the train loss stood at an impressive 0.0478, while the validation loss settled at 0.2222.
Moreover, our model achieved a remarkable train accuracy of 0.9847, demonstrating its efficacy in learning from the training data.
Similarly, the validation accuracy reached a commendable level of 0.9415, indicating the model's ability to perform well on previously unseen data.
